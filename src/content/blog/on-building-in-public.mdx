I spend a lot of time reading and bookmarking, but the real growth happens when I building and writing. This site is a space to move from collecting to exploring.

The name cyber-rhizome comes from plants like ginger or grass. They don’t have a central trunk or a hierarchy; they grow horizontally, connecting node to node. Instead of rigid categories, this site reflects the way I think: a horizontal network where tags provide the connections.

## 🧪 The Mix
This is a digital lab notebook for whatever I’m working on:

- Systems Architecture, AI, TypeScript, Python, Laravel
- Fermentation, Pickling, Gardening
- Sewing & Maker Projects

## 🧵 Current Thread
Right now, most of my energy is going into sentinel-l7. It’s an AI-driven compliance engine built on Laravel, Vue 3, and Upstash Redis Streams. It’s been a fascinating engineering challenge, and I’ll be posting technical deep dives as I navigate the architecture.
